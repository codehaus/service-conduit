Welcome

Service Symphony is an enterprise architecture consultancy with a proven track record in delivering end-to-end IT solutions for a variety of financial and real estate customers in Europe and the Middle East. Our customer base includes major players in European retail banking clearing sector, worldwide leader in currency exchange, and prominent real estate services in the Middle East.


We also are the active mainainers of SCA4J, an enterprise application framework for building service oriented applications based on OASIS OpenCSA suite of specifications on SCA (Service Component Architecture). SCA and SCA4J enable you to build large-scale enterprise class service oriented applications as loosely coupled services. SCA4J is available as open source with a business friendly license (ASL 2.0)


Service Symphony consultants have significant experience in full SDLC and are industry experts in Java, JEE and SOA-related technologies. We also have proven expertise in the financial clearing and settlement domain, especially around SEPA (Single European Payment Area)
